KitKat Chocolatory Project

Photography of my old living places.

These are some photogrpah in Tsuen Wan Cheung Shan Estate. I spent a lot of time here in my childhood. My all grandparents are living in this estate and there is also my parents old home so I often visit there when there are festivals. The architecture structure of the building is just like a well. When I was small , I always find it scary to look down from 17/f even my view was blocked by the field, but when I grow up and return to this place there is nothing block my view again , and I am not fear about the height anymore. I tried to quoted down in order to use a child perspective to take these photos and some view was blocked by fields .It is a project that provide me a chance to re-visit the old place where full of memories. Although some of my grandparents have gone , those memories are still exists in every corner. It was a place that can strike the chord in my heart.

  • Date:December 2018
  • Category: Photography
